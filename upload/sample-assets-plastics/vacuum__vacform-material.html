Vacuum Forming

Many types of thermoplastics are suitable for vacuum forming. The most popular is high impact polystyrene (HIPS). It is relatively cheap, comes in a wide range of colours and is easy to form. Typical thicknesses of vacuum forming sheet material used range from less than 1mm up to 6mm.

Some thermoplastics (e.g. ABS) require pre-drying in an oven before vacuum forming. If this is not done, moisture blisters may appear on the surface when the material is heated.
